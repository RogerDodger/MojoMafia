Exeuction rules for actions
===========================

1. Actions of dead or silenced players do not execute.
2. Actions of equal predence execute "simlutaneously", e.g., serial killers targetting each other will both die.

Action precedence
-----------------

 : Hooker, Drunkard
 : Doctor, Bodyguard
 : Serial Killer
 : Goon
 : Vigilante
 : Cop, Tracker, Watcher, ...
